Unicef Tap Project

Webdesign and concept for a Facebook application which lets users make a $5 donation ,via paypal or text message, and then turn their Facebook profile into a ‘Tap’. This allowed them to pass the water on to two friends who can open their Taps and pass the water on to their friends and so on. The user can watch his own water network grow within the overall network. In addition celebrities and ambassadors like Alyssa Milano, Nas, Jeff Bridges, Kevin Spacey, Heidi Klum among others opened their own Taps and invited their fans to join via Facebook and Twitter. Realized at Droga5
